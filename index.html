Future Career

I have always wanted a career in the IT industry since leaving secondary school but wasn't sure what sector to choose. I've always found the idea of building websites fascinating so decided to research about how it happens, developing a dream of becoming a full-stack developer along the way. Over a year ago, I was introduced to a senior full-stack developer who told me about his experience and career in web development and how I could make a career from it too.


He introduced me to the Frontend Codecademy course and a developer roadmap which I am treating like a job until completion, knowing I'm close to nailing the basics of HTML, CSS, JavaScript and now making a start on React, I have confidence in myself to be able build a strong responsive and interactive Frontend website.


I can't wait to make a start on the Founders & Coders course to make the next big step in my web development career path. The opportunity to learn full-stack web development and to gain real-world experience sounds amazing to me. I am fully committed to achieving my dream of becoming a senior full-stack web developer.


I feel like Founders and Coders is the right place for me to train because I was advised to take this course by a senior full-stack web developer which I met who also introduced me to a Frontend coding bootcamp course over a year ago. Founders and Coders are providing a fantastic opportunity which I am fully committed to reaching the next big step in achieving my dream of becoming a full-stack web developer. I will contribute by bringing a positive and supportive attitude towards everyone involved in the Founders and Coders community so everyone feels safe and in high spirits.
